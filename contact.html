FAQ

Common
Questions

Are courses available online?

Yes — all courses are available via live Zoom sessions and recorded on-demand materials, plus in-person at our Cairo facility.

Do I receive a certificate upon completion?

Every graduate receives an IMP-accredited certificate. PMP and SHRM programs also carry internationally recognized credentials.

Is corporate training available?

Yes. We offer fully customized in-house programs tailored to your organization's specific goals. Contact us to discuss.

What languages are courses taught in?

Most courses are available in both Arabic and English. Language of instruction is specified on each course's detail page.

How quickly will you respond?

Our enrollment advisors respond within 24 hours on working days (Sun–Thu, Cairo time).
